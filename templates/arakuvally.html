ARAKU VALLY

Araku Valley is a hill station in the Vishakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh. It's known for its:
Biodiversity, Coffee plantations, Tribal culture, Waterfalls, Forests, Climate
Araku Valley is also known as the Ooty of Andhra Pradesh. It's a popular destination for:

Nature lovers
Adventure enthusiasts
Tourists visiting Vizag
