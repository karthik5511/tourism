AMARAVATHI

Amaravati is a popular tourist destination in Andhra Pradesh. It's located 32 kilometers north of Guntur District.
The city is known for its historical, religious, and cultural heritage.
It was the capital of the Satavahana Dynasty in the 2nd Century BCE

Amaravati Stupa, Archaeological Museum, Amareswara Temple, Mahachaitya Amaravathi,
Champions Yacht Club, Mogalarajpuram Caves, Undavalli Caves.
The banks of the Krishna River are also a popular picnic spot.
