Tirumala is a pilgrimage center in the Chittoor district of Andhra.
It's home to the Tirupati Balaji Temple, also known as the Tirumala Temple or Tirupati Temple.
The temple is located on the seventh peak of the Tirumala Hills, which are 3,200 feet high.
The seven peaks represent the seven hoods of Adisesha, the serpent under which Vishnu is said to reside.

Tirumala is one of the most visited pilgrimage centers in the world.
It's also one of the world's richest temples.
Millions of devotees visit the temple every year.
